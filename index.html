Hi, I’m a Ph.D. candidate in Kamijo Laboratory, school of Information Science and Technology, the University of Tokyo. I'm now working on deeplearning based image processing system for autonomous driving, supervised by Prof. Shunsuke Kamijo.
Since Dec. 2020, I'm a research intern in 3D Vision&Graphic Group, HuaWei Tokyo Research Center, supervised by Dr. Bo Zheng.

I received my M.E. from Graduate School of Information, Production and Systems, Waseda Universiy, supervised by Prof. Takeshi Ikenaga, in 2020. I received my B.E. from School of Instrument Science and Engineering, Southeast Universiy, in 2019.
